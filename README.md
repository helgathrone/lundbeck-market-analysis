# Antidepressant Market Analysis: Strategic Insights for Lundbeck

This project explores the European antidepressant market with a focus on the strategic positioning of two key Lundbeck products: **Brintellix (Trintellix)** and **Cipralex (Lexapro)**.

The analysis combines epidemiological indicators, pharmaceutical consumption data, market dynamics, and company revenue structure to identify key trends and potential strategic opportunities.

---

# Project Objective

The primary goal of this analysis is to understand how trends in **depression burden, antidepressant consumption, and pharmaceutical market dynamics** influence the strategic positioning of Lundbeck’s antidepressant portfolio.

Special attention is given to:

- the growth trajectory of **Brintellix**
- the role of **Cipralex as a mature originator brand**
- potential expansion opportunities in emerging markets

---

# Data Sources

The project integrates multiple datasets:

- **DALYs (Disability-Adjusted Life Years)** for depression  
- **Antidepressant consumption data (DDD)**  
- **Pharmaceutical market sales data (PPP-adjusted)**  
- **Lundbeck revenue data extracted from company reports (PDF tables)**  

Additionally, external sources were referenced to provide market context, including reports on the growth of antidepressant demand in Ukraine.

---

# Analysis Structure

The notebook is organized into the following sections:

1. Depression burden overview (DALYs)  
2. Regional differences in depression burden  
3. Antidepressant consumption trends over time  
4. Relationship between depression burden and medication consumption  
5. Market landscape across countries  
6. Focus markets analysis (Italy and Spain)  
7. Identification of emerging markets  
8. Ukraine case study  
9. Lundbeck revenue structure analysis  
10. Exploratory machine learning experiment (logistic regression)

---

# Key Insights

Several important patterns emerge from the analysis:

- Depression burden remains substantial and is increasing in some regions, suggesting long-term demand for treatment.
- Antidepressant consumption has generally increased over time and shows a positive relationship with depression burden.
- Significant differences exist between national markets due to healthcare systems, prescribing practices, and access to treatment.
- Within Lundbeck’s portfolio, **Brintellix continues to demonstrate strong growth and acts as a key strategic product**.
- **Cipralex shows declining revenue due to generic competition**, but retains value as the original escitalopram formulation with strong clinical credibility.

---

# Strategic Opportunity: Ukraine

The Ukrainian market may represent a promising long-term expansion opportunity.

Key drivers include:

- increasing depression burden, particularly after 2022
- rapidly growing demand for antidepressants (reported growth of **72% over two years**)
- the inclusion of **escitalopram in the national reimbursement program in 2024**

Together, these factors suggest that expanding the presence of both **Brintellix** and **Cipralex** in Ukraine could represent a viable strategic opportunity.

# Limitations

This project is based on publicly available aggregated datasets. The analysis does not incorporate detailed factors such as pricing strategies, physician prescribing behavior, marketing investments, or competitive positioning of specific generic manufacturers.

Therefore, the findings should be interpreted as **exploratory strategic insights rather than precise market forecasts**.
